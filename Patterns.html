Here's an Easy Pattern to Try!

Tutorials History of Knitting Patterns

For an easy scarf pattern try this:

Cast on as many stitches onto your left needle for the desired length of the scarf.
Knit across the first row.(This will be your right side or 'rs' in many knitting patterns)
Purl across the second row. (This will be your wrong side or 'ws')
Repeat knitting and purling on the appropriate sides for the desired length of the scarf.
Once you have reached the desired length of the scarf go ahead and cast off.
Wear your new cozy scarf proudly!

The Purl Stitch

Tutorials History of Knitting Patterns

The second step is learning the basic purl stitch. Insert your right needle through the top of the loop and exiting through the front of the left needle. Wrap the yarn around the needle counter clockwise. Take your right needle and go back up the loop on your left needle that you originally went though and take that newly wrapped loop along with you. Then slip the old loop off the needle. Congratulations! You have purled your first stitch.
